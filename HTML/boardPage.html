PLAYERS

Player 1: (WASD)

Player 2: (IJKL)



RULES

1. Chose who will be player 1 and player 2

2. Move your character where you want to start on the board

3. Player 1 will go first; they will place one of their tiles onto the board - then Player 2 will go

4. Take turns

5. If your piece moves off the board, you lose

6. To start a new game, click the "NEW GAME" button
